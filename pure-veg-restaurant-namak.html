VEDIK RESORT · DINING

NAMAK

Pure Vegetarian Fine Dining

From slow-cooked Gujarati thali to contemporary plant-based cuisine, NAMAK honours the Vedic tradition of sattvic eating — pure, nourishing, and thoughtfully prepared from our own organic gardens.

Every dish at NAMAK tells a story of our land, our culture, and our commitment to pure vegetarian excellence. Open for breakfast, lunch and dinner — guests and visitors welcome.

🌿 Farm to Fork · Open Daily · 100% Pure Veg
OUR PHILOSOPHY

Sattvic Eating,
Elevated Experience

At NAMAK, we believe food is sacred. Rooted in the Vedic philosophy of sattvic cooking — food that is pure, clean, and life-giving — every meal is prepared with intention, using organic produce grown right on our resort grounds.

Our chefs blend traditional Gujarati recipes with contemporary presentation, creating a dining experience that nourishes both body and soul. No onion, no garlic — and yet, no compromise on flavour.

MENU HIGHLIGHTS
