BANQUET OPTIONS

Extraordinary Venues for Every Celebration

From marigold-lit lawns under the stars to opulent indoor halls — every setting is designed to make your event unforgettable.

Madhuvan Lawn

🎊 Up to 1,500 Guests

Our largest and most spectacular venue — the Madhuvan Lawn is a sprawling outdoor expanse perfect for grand weddings and large celebrations. The natural landscape provides a breathtaking backdrop for an unforgettable event.

Grand Lawn

🎊 500–1,000 Guests

A versatile outdoor lawn ideal for mid-to-large events. Beautifully landscaped with ambient lighting options, this venue adapts seamlessly to your vision — from traditional ceremonies to contemporary celebrations.

Vedik Banquet Hall

🏛 200–800 Guests

Our elegant indoor banquet hall combines Ethno-Modern interiors with full-service amenities. Climate-controlled and fully equipped, it's perfect for formal receptions, corporate events, and celebrations in all seasons.
