Madhuvan Lawn

Madhuvan Lawn is our premier outdoor event venue — a breathtaking expanse of manicured grounds set against the natural landscape of the Vedik Resort. It is the ideal canvas for grand weddings, large family celebrations, and high-profile corporate events.

As the sun sets and fairy lights illuminate the marigold-adorned space, Madhuvan Lawn transforms into a magical, unforgettable setting. Our team of event specialists ensure every detail is attended to — from the floral arrangements to the lighting, catering, and décor.
