Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Vedik Resort (a unit of Four Seasons Inns & Suites Pvt. Ltd.) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a booking.
