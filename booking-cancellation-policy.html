Booking & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Booking Policy

All reservations are subject to room availability. A valid credit card or advance payment may be required to guarantee your booking. Confirmation will be sent to the email address provided at the time of booking.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 7 or more days prior to arrival will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days of arrival may be subject to a one-night cancellation charge. No-shows will be charged the full reservation amount.

Event & Wedding Bookings

Event and wedding bookings require a non-refundable deposit. Specific terms and conditions will be outlined in your event contract. Please contact our reservations team for details.
