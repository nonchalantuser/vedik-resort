OUR STORY

Vedik Resort
(A Unit of Four Seasons Inns
& Suites Pvt. Ltd.)

With the ideal 'Atithi Devo Bhav', VEDIK RESORT is a hidden gem in a lap of nature about 50 minutes from Surat International Airport. The world-class luxury resort blends in completely with the uniqueness of the landscape.

It combines tremendous natural beauty and outdoor fun with impeccable indoor luxury. The Resort extends up to 10 acres and provides modern amenities and lavish accommodation in an Ethno-Modern set up. We offer 176 luxuriously appointed rooms & suites, located amidst serene natural environs with the guarantee of perfect luxury & privacy for our discerning business & leisure guests.

We provide our guests with experiences to cherish for a life-time. A perfect destination for rejuvenating weekends, family vacations, corporate retreats, and destination weddings.
